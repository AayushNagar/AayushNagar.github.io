$ about

Aayush Nagar is a software engineer and engineering leader based in Glasgow, with over a decade in banking and financial services. He's a Vice President of Software Engineering at JPMorganChase, working in wholesale credit risk.

His focus is putting machine learning and generative AI into the hands of the people who need it — reliable model access on AWS Bedrock, agentic solutions, and analytics built on large-scale data in Databricks. In short: turning data and new AI capabilities into tools people can actually use.
