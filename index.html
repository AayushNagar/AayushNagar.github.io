02 about

I'm a software engineer and engineering leader based in Glasgow, UK, with over a decade in banking and financial services. For the last seven-plus years I've been at JPMorganChase in the Wholesale Credit Risk and Analytics team, where I'm currently a Vice President of Software Engineering.

Our team's work has grown from analytics, into machine learning, and now into large language models and agentic AI. Day to day I'm the engineer who connects platforms, models and people — giving data scientists reliable access to models on AWS Bedrock, building GenAI and agentic solutions on top of large-scale data in Databricks, and delivering analytics as BAU.

A lot of what I build serves credit and middle-office teams who need to understand the credit-risk world better — turning massive datasets and new AI capabilities into tools people can actually use.
